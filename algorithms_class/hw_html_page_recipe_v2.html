Simple Deviled Eggs



The eggs are delicious, and they are easy to make. You can add onions and celery for a little more flavor and texture.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs
  • 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
  • 3 tablespoon of relish
  • 1/2 teaspoon of mustard
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Coarse salt
  • Paprika, for serving
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped onion(optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of finely chopped celery(optional)

Directions

  1. Place eggs in a medium saucepan; add cold water to cover by 1 inch. Bring to a rolling boil. Remove pan from heat; cover, and let stand 7 minutes. Drain,
    and run eggs under cold water to cool.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together mayonnaise, relish, mustard, a large pinch of cayenne pepper, and optional the two optional ingredients if desire.
  3. Peel eggs, and halve lengthwise. Carefully remove yolks to bowl with mayonnaise mixture, leaving whites intact.
  4. Mash egg yolks with mayonnaise mixture until smooth; season with salt to taste. Mound into egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika if desire.
